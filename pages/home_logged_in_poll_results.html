Confirmit’s demo panel and demo panel portal has been created to provide an example of a portal created in Confirmit’ Professional and Standard panel offering. The design here is only intended as an example of what can be created using the Panel Portal designer. You have full flexibility to create a panel portal to fit your needs, in terms of both functionality and look and feel.
If you have the need to leverage a fully developed CMS system to create and manage your panel portal, you can use the Panel Portal Flex API provided by Confirmit to integrate the panel features into the external panel portal.
