Frequently Asked Questions
What is a FAQ?
FAQ is pronounced as separate letters, or as "fak," and short for frequently asked questions, a FAQ is an online document that poses a series of common questions and answers on a specific topic.
What can I use this page for?
You can use this page to present the panelist with answers to frequently asked questions relating to your panel or the use of the portal.
